My Favorite

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

The movie is an American animated superhero
film released on December 14, 2018. The film's
story follows the Marvel Comics character
Miles Morales / Spider-Man as he teams up
with other Spider-People from different
dimensions to save New York City.

Smurfs: The Lost Village

In this fully animated, all-new take on the
Smurfs, a mysterious map sets Smurfette
and her friends Brainy, Clumsy, and Hefty
on an exciting race through the Forbidden
Forest, leading to the discovery of the
biggest secret in Smurf history.

Detective Pikachu

In a world where people collect Pokémon to
do battle, a boy comes across an intelligent
talking Pikachu who seeks to be a detective.
The story begins when ace detective Harry
Goodman goes mysteriously missing,
prompting his 21-year-old son Tim to find out
what happened.
